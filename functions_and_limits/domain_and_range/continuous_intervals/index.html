Continuous Intervals

Intervals are a way of expressing a collection of numbers.

The interval \( \lbrack 1, 3 \rbrack \) is closed. This means that it includes the endpoints, i.e. the numbers \(1\) and \(3\).

A beautiful picture of a number line.

The interval \( ( 1, 3 ) \) is open. This means that it includes the endpoints, i.e. the numbers \(1\) and \(3\).
